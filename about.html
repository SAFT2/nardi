About Nardi's Beauty Shop

Our Story

Nardi, a passionate entrepreneur from Addis Ababa, founded Nardi’s Beauty Shop in 2020 to empower women through beauty. With a vision to bring high-quality cosmetics, human hair, and services to Ethiopia, Nardi has built a haven where every woman can feel confident and beautiful.

Our Mission

To provide premium beauty products and services that celebrate individuality, enhance natural beauty, and inspire self-love.
