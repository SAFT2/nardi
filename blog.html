Top 5 Makeup Trends in Ethiopia 2025

Ethiopia’s beauty scene is evolving, blending traditional vibes with modern flair. Here are the top 5 makeup trends to watch in 2025:

1. Bold Brows

Thick, defined brows are taking center stage. Think natural shapes with a touch of gel for that polished, effortless look.

2. Golden Glow

Highlighters in rich gold tones are popping off, especially on Ethiopia’s warm skin tones. Dab it on your cheekbones for a radiant vibe.

3. Vivid Lips

Deep reds and corals are stealing the show, inspired by local fabrics and festive energy. Pair with a matte finish for all-day wear.

4. Graphic Liners

Sharp, artistic eyeliner designs are trending among Addis’s bold fashionistas. From sleek wings to double lines, it’s all about making a statement.

5. Minimal Base

Dewy, light foundations are in for a fresh, everyday glow. Let your natural beauty shine with just a touch of tint.

How to Care for Human Hair Extensions

Human hair extensions can elevate your style, but they need love to stay flawless. Here’s how to keep them looking their best:

1. Gentle Washing

Use sulfate-free shampoo and lukewarm water. Wash them gently, avoiding tangles, and rinse thoroughly.

2. Condition Regularly

Apply a hydrating conditioner from mid-lengths to ends. This keeps them soft and prevents dryness in Addis’s climate.

3. Brush with Care

Use a wide-tooth comb or loop brush, starting from the ends and working up. Patience prevents breakage.

4. Heat Protection

Use a heat protectant spray before styling. Keep heat low to preserve the hair’s natural shine.

5. Store Properly

Hang your extensions or lay them flat in a silk bag when not in use to prevent matting.

Choosing the Perfect Perfume

Finding a scent that matches your personality is an art. Here’s your guide to picking the perfect perfume:

1. Know Your Notes

Perfumes have top, middle, and base notes. Love floral? Go for rose or jasmine. Crave warmth? Try oud or sandalwood.

2. Test on Your Skin

Spritz it on your wrist and wait 10 minutes—your skin chemistry changes the scent. Paper strips don’t cut it.

3. Match the Mood

Light and citrusy for day, deep and musky for night. Pick a scent that fits your Addis vibe.

4. Layer Smartly

Use matching lotions or unscented bases to boost longevity. Avoid clashing scents—keep it harmonious.

5. Trust Your Instinct

If it makes you feel confident and happy, it’s the one. Perfume is personal—go with what speaks to you.
